About

Chess Enthusiast is a product of the Central Washington University Chess Club in Ellensburg, Washington. It was founded by Loren I. P. Sum in the fall of 2011. Chess Enthusiast strives to be your premier internet destination for Chess.

Club Information

The CWU Chess Club is at the core of Chess Enthusiast. Come join us for some fun!
